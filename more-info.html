How often will you deliver?

Right now, we're aiming for an episode a month. The word aiming is key.

Can I pitch a kitchen for an episode?

Please do. We're slightly limited geographically (we're based in cities on the East Coast), but we travel for fun. We want to work with listeners to tell stories about eating in your hometown, wherever that may be. If you want to recommend a place, email us with the following info:

  • The name, location and contact information for the restaurant
  • What you like about the establishment

Who the heck are you?

Friends who met at a college newspaper and bonded over many things. We catch up best with food and beers in front of us.
